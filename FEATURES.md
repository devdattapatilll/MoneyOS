# Features

## Core Capabilities

- **Smart Categorization**: 14 categories with fuzzy matching
- **Leak Detection**: Subscriptions, BNPL, food delivery tracking
- **Wellness Score**: 0-100 financial health rating
- **Visual Analytics**: Interactive charts with Recharts
- **Anomaly Detection**: Flags unusual spending patterns

## Categories

Food, Shopping, Transport, Bills, Healthcare, Entertainment, Travel, Subscriptions, BNPL, EMI, Investments, Income, Transfer, Miscellaneous
